How to Earn Points
Capture Turfs
Go to a turf location and press E to start a takeover. Defend it until the timer runs out to earn points.
Defend Turfs
Keeping control of turfs earns your gang passive points over time. The more turfs you control, the more points you'll earn.
Grow Your Gang
Having more active members helps defend turfs and capture new ones. Recruitment is key to success!
